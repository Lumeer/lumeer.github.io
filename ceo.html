All tables at one place

Table with business trips at accountant, table with wages at the assistant, table with commissions at the sales director – everybody creates tables nowadays. And everybody uses a different format. In Lumeer, you get them all at a single place, connect with relations and clean up the data.

When you want to update your chart in Excel or a calendar, you need to search for the source data first, and then modify them. In Lumeer, you can edit the chart or calendar directly. The source data are updated automatically.
