What is Lumeer?

Lumeer can help you in situations where

  • you look for another specialized application to handle your daily routines,
  • you have a bunch of records that you need to process,
  • you are about to create yet another Excel or Google Sheets document.

How many hours do you spend weekly dealing with some data records, putting information in the right fields or cells, copying and moving values, creating reports? Don't you feel like this is the same task all again and again? We noticed recurring patterns when working with business data or records even accros various industry fields. We believe we can make your job much easier and save you precious time that you can possibly spend with your family.

Lumeer is like a friend who can help you with your tasks so your kids can enjoy you more.

We simply allow you to input your data in the form you want to see them, make sure we understand their meaning, allow you to specify your own processes that need to be followed, and in the end we offer you wide variety of views of the same data. These views can be directly used to input your data and serve as reports as well.
